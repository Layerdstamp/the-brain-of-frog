We feed you leads, you pay $X/month, plus you get to use the MonitorMyAir brand on your trucks, ads, and yard signs. We don't compete; we deliver the qualified buyer."*

### Why a chain will pay $1,000-$5,000/mo for this
- They spend $5k-$20k/mo on Google Ads with sub-10% close rates
- You're handing them branded credibility (MonitorMyAir is 10x more trustworthy than "Acme Mold")
- The AI library answers customer questions 24/7 (their phone doesn't ring at 2am)
- The expert reports pre-qualify everyone who lands a job with them

### How to close it in one call
- Target: top 2-3 remediation operators in Fort Wayne / Indy (same list as [02-contractor-partners.md](./02-contractor-partners.md))
- After they say yes to the referral fee partnership, upsell:
  > "Different conversation: I'm thinking about granting exclusive metro licensing rights to one operator in each Indiana market. Fort Wayne is open. Pricing is $2,500/month for exclusive lead routing and brand co-licensing in this metro. Want to talk before I open it to the others?"
- Even partial close (say $1,000/mo) = $12k/year recurring

### Timeline: 30-60 days to first close. Recurring revenue from day 1 of close.

## Sidecar B — One commercial coatings / paint contract ($25k-$80k single job)

You said it explicitly: *"I'll do a $30k paint job on a commercial building."* You have the LLC, insurance, license. Most commercial painters in Fort Wayne are sub-scale operators.

### Where the $30k-$80k jobs are
1. **Property management firms managing commercial real estate** in Allen County — they always need exterior paint refreshes, parking lot striping, dumpster enclosures, etc.
2. **Apartment complex turn-units** — paint 30 turn-units per quarter for the complex's property manager = $500-$1,200/unit × 30 = $15k-$36k single PO
3. **Industrial floor coatings** — epoxy floors for warehouses, breweries, body shops. $5-$10/sq ft × 3,000 sq ft = $15k-$30k single job
4. **HOA exterior paint contracts** — 20-30 units in a townhome HOA = $40k-$120k

### How to land the first one in 30 days
- Pull list of Fort Wayne commercial property managers (Google "commercial property management Fort Wayne" + Allen County tax records for commercial owners)
- Send the email below to 25 firms
- Realistic: 2-3 invite a quote, 1 closes within 60 days

### Cold email template
```
Subject: Open availability for [exterior repaint / floor coatings / unit turns] — Fort Wayne

[First name] —

Alex Yeskolski, [Your LLC]. Licensed and insured commercial contractor 
in Fort Wayne. Currently have a Q[X] crew availability window for one 
commercial repaint or coatings project in the $25k-$80k range.

Specialties:
  - Exterior commercial repaints (single buildings or multi-unit)
  - Industrial floor coatings (epoxy, polyaspartic)
  - Apartment turn-unit interior paint (30+ unit POs)
  - Fast turnaround, single-source crew, COI on request

If you have anything in scope this quarter, I'd be glad to walk the 
property and bid it within 5 business days.

[Your name]
[Phone] | [Email]
COI / W-9 / License # on request.
```

### Timeline: First walk-through in 14-21 days, first close in 30-60 days, first PO clears in 60-90 days.
