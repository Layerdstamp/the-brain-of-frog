# Target Lists — Research Templates

I built three CSVs in this folder:
- `targets-contractors.csv` — 15 Indiana mold remediation companies (12 franchises + 3 independents)
- `targets-attorneys.csv` — Indiana property/insurance attorneys to research
- `targets-agents.csv` — Top Fort Wayne / Indy realtors

## Why every field says VERIFY

I don't fabricate phone numbers. The companies on `targets-contractors.csv` are real national/regional brands with high probability of having Indiana franchise locations. Phone numbers and franchise-owner names change yearly — Alex (or I, with web access in next session) needs to verify each one before outreach.

## Fastest way to verify

For each contractor row, 60 seconds in Google:
1. Search `{{company}} {{city}} Indiana`
2. Pull phone + owner name from Google Business Profile + the "About" page
3. Pull email from the "Contact" page (often `info@`, `{{firstname}}@`, or a web form)
4. Paste into the CSV

10 contractors × 60 sec = 10 minutes to a clean call list.

## If Alex grants Reddit / Facebook access

I can also pull contractor reputation from r/Indianapolis, r/FortWayne, and local "homeowner help" Facebook groups — gives us a sort order (which contractors get praised vs. flagged) so we pitch the GOOD ones first.

## Sort order for cold-call sequence

When verifying, mark each as A/B/C in the Status column:
- **A:** Independent (not franchise) — they keep more margin, more motivated to take referral fees.
- **B:** Single-location franchise — owner is usually involved in operations, can decide same call.
- **C:** Multi-location regional chain — slower decision, but bigger deal sizes.

**Pitch A's first.** Mondays through Wednesdays. Goal: 3 signed agreements in 14 days.
