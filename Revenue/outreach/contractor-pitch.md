# Contractor Partner Outreach — Ready to Send

**Use:** First contact with Indiana mold remediation contractors. Pick from phone-call script OR cold email. Don't do both on first contact.

---

## PHONE CALL SCRIPT (4 minutes, highest conversion)

**Best time to call:** Tue–Thu, 10am–11am or 2pm–3pm local. Skip Mondays and Fridays.

---

**[Receptionist or owner answers]**

> "Hi, this is Alex with MonitorMyAir over in Fort Wayne. Could I speak with the owner or whoever handles new project intake? It's not a sales call — I'm a building inspector and I want to refer paid jobs to you."

**[Owner takes call]**

> "Hey {{Name}}, Alex Yeskolski with MonitorMyAir and Investigative Inspection Services. I'll be quick. I'm a licensed inspector and I write expert mold protocols for homeowners — they pay me $497 to $2,000 to assess their problem and write a scope-of-work document. After that, they need a contractor. Right now I'm sending those homeowners to 2-3 of the better remediation companies in the state, and I'd like to add you to that list. Are you the right person to talk to about that?"

**[If yes, continue:]**

> "Here's how it works. Homeowner pays me. I write the protocol. I send them 3 vetted contractors — you'd be one. They pick. If you close the job, you pay me 10% as a marketing fee. If you don't close it, it cost you nothing. I'm only working with one contractor per market on this, so I'm trying to find the right partner.
>
> Three quick questions to see if we're a fit:
>
> 1. **What's your typical residential remediation ticket size?** (Looking for $5k-$30k jobs.)
> 2. **Are you IICRC-certified or have S520-trained techs?** (My protocols are written to S520, so it matters.)
> 3. **What's your usual lead time from estimate to start?** (Faster = more likely to win the homeowner.)"

**[Listen. If answers reasonable, go to close:]**

> "Okay, sounds like a fit. I'd like to send you a 1-page referral agreement — non-exclusive on your side, simple terms, 10% on closed work, paid net-15 after the homeowner pays you. Can I send it to {{email}}? I'll have a first lead in the pipeline within 2 weeks."

**[Close. End of call.]**

## FOLLOW-UP CADENCE

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| 0 | First call OR email |
| 3 | If no response: email follow-up — "circling back, want me to send the 1-pager?" |
| 7 | If no response: second call OR LinkedIn DM |
| 14 | Final follow-up: "moving forward with another contractor in your market — last chance" |
| 21 | Move on. They're not the right partner. |

**Goal: 3 signed agreements within 14 days.** Pitch 10-15 contractors to land 3.
