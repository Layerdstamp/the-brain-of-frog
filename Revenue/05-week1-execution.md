| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|

| You over-engineer the landing page instead of shipping | Use the file I'm giving you. Don't redesign. Ship. |
| You take a Tier 1 customer's $497 and don't deliver the report in 48 hrs | Block 90 min on Day 5 for the FIRST one. Build template after #2. |
| Indiana licensing issue (you can't call it an "inspection") | Re-read [01-offer-design.md](./01-offer-design.md) "What you DON'T do" section. Use "expert opinion report" / "protocol". Verify with Indiana attorney general / IDOI if unsure. |
| Contractor partner doesn't pay commission | Agreement Section 3 + 4 protects you. Drop them if they slow-pay; sign two new ones. |
