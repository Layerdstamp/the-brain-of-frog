## 7. Cleaning Protocol

1. HEPA vacuum all surfaces within containment, including framing, joists, subfloor.
2. Damp-wipe all non-porous surfaces with EPA-registered antimicrobial per label directions.
3. HEPA vacuum AGAIN after damp-wipe has dried.
4. Air scrub the contained area with HEPA filtration for minimum 24 hours after final cleaning.

## 8. Post-Remediation Verification (Required Before Reconstruction)

- Visual inspection: no visible growth, debris, or residue.
- Moisture content: all retained wood framing ≤16% MC.
- **Independent post-remediation clearance:** REQUIRED. Performed by MonitorMyAir (included if Tier 3 purchased; $497 add-on for Tier 1/2 customers). Contractor may not invoice final payment until clearance issued.

## 9. Reconstruction (Out of Scope for Remediation Contractor)

Drywall replacement, paint, flooring, trim — typically handled by a general contractor AFTER clearance. Do not bundle with remediation bid; separate trade.
