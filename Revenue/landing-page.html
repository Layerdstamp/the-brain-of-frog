Frequently asked

Is this a mold inspection?

No. It's an expert opinion report — a written professional analysis and remediation protocol. Inspections are a different regulated category. Our reports are designed to be more useful in negotiation, insurance claims, and legal disputes than typical inspection reports.

How is this different from a free contractor estimate?

Contractors writing free estimates are also writing the scope of work they want to be paid for. Ours is independent — you pay us once, then use the doc to compare any contractor's bid.

Do you do the remediation work?

No. We do the assessment + protocol only. We have vetted contractor partners we can refer you to (and we're transparent: partners pay us a referral fee on closed work — disclosed in your engagement letter — but that fee comes from them, not from you).

Will my insurance company accept this?

Our Standard Protocol and Full On-Site reports are written in the format adjusters expect. We can't guarantee an outcome, but most carriers prefer a documented third-party protocol over a single contractor's word.

What if I'm outside Indiana?

Tier 1 (Triage) and Tier 2 (Standard Protocol) are 100% remote — anywhere in the US. Tier 3 (Full On-Site) requires Allen County, Indiana + 60 miles.

How fast?

Triage report: 48 hours from your consult. Standard Protocol: 48-72 hours. Full On-Site: 5-7 days from booking (depending on travel scheduling).
